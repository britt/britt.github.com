One-sentence summaries of each cocktail recipe in the [Cocktail Recipes](/cocktails/) collection.

## Recipes

- [Anderson Island Sunset](/cocktails/anderson-island-sunset/): A tequila drink with guava nectar, sparkling water, margarita mix, mint, and frozen blueberries.
- [Bitter Nonsense](/cocktails/bitter-nonsense/): A Negroni-adjacent mix of Campari and sweet vermouth, topped with soda water and finished with orange.
- [Bittersweet Symphony](/cocktails/bittersweet-symphony/): A bourbon Paloma-style cocktail that uses fresh grapefruit juice with orange liqueur, lime, and orange bitters.
- [El Niño](/cocktails/el-nino/): A tequila cocktail combining pear liqueur, lime juice, allspice dram, simple syrup, and grapefruit bitters.
- [Envejeciendo](/cocktails/envejeciendo/): A mezcal and grapefruit drink with orange liqueur, maple syrup, lime juice, Tajín, cayenne, and bitters.
- [Holiday Spiced Sour](/cocktails/holiday-spiced-sour/): A bourbon sour-style drink with maple syrup, lemon, apple cider vinegar, and a mix of holiday spices and bitters.
- [Johnny Appleseed](/cocktails/johnny-appleseed/): An Applejack riff on a Rattlesnake that uses maple syrup, lemon juice, egg white, and bitters.
- [La Pomme Forte](/cocktails/la-pomme-forte/): An Applejack cocktail with lemon, St. Germain, allspice dram, brown sugar syrup, and a splash of soda.
- [Mother of Invention](/cocktails/mother-of-invention/): A "use what you have" cocktail built around orange curaçao and Cara Cara orange juice with allspice, cayenne, and bitters.
- [Protein G1](/cocktails/protein-g1/): A blended drink made from AG1, vanilla whey protein powder, and water.
- [Slightly Sour Strawberry Smash](/cocktails/slightly-sour-strawberry-smash/): A strawberry old fashioned that balances sweetness with black walnut cordial and Angostura bitters.
- [That's Just Dandy](/cocktails/thats-just-dandy/): A bourbon cocktail shaken with lemon juice, apricot preserves, simple syrup, Angostura, and mint.
- [Yippee Kay Yay Motherfucker!](/cocktails/yippee-kay-yay-motherfucker/): A spicy Old Fashioned variant mixing bourbon and maple syrup with Angostura, cayenne, and a lemon twist.
