A whiskey sour that raided the spice cabinet at Christmas.

* 2 oz BOURBON
* ¾ oz MAPLE SYRUP
* ½ oz APPLE CIDER VINEGAR
* ½ oz LEMON JUICE
* 3 DASH AROMATIC BITTERS
* 1 PINCH CINNAMON
* 1 PINCH ALLSPICE
* 1 PINCH CLOVES
* ½ PINCH NUTMEG
* 1 PINCH CAYENNE

Mix everything in a shaker with ice and shake hard
